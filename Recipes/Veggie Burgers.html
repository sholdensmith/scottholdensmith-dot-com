Veggie Burgers

Ingredients

3 cup lentils

10 tsp salt

3 cup bulgur

1/4 cup oil

8 cups finely chopped onion

2 cup finely chopped celery

2 cup finely chopped leek

8 tsp minced garlic

26 cups sliced mushrooms

4 cups raw unsalted cashews

1-1/3 cups mayo

8 cups panko

Pepper

Directions

3 cup lentils (cook with 12 cup water, 4 tsp salt, drain, and spread on paper towels)
3 cup bulgur (bring 8 cups water to boil with 2 tsp salt, add bulgar, cover, let stand off heat, drain, press out moisture, sit in bowl)
1/4 cup oil
8 cups finely chopped onion
2 cup finely chopped celery
2 cup finely chopped leek
8 tsp minced garlic

(Cook, then spread on baking sheet to cool)
1/4 cup oil
26 cups sliced mushrooms

(Cook until golden brown, and spread out with veggies)
4 cups raw unsalted cashews (process using 15 1-sec pulses, then put in bowl with bulgar and lentils, veggies and 1-1/3 cup mayo)
Process this mixture in food processor with 20 1-sec pulses, and mix in 8 cups panko, 4 tsp salt and pepper)
