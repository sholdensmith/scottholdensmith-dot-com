Burritos

Ingredients

Ground beef or impossible burger meat


Cooked beans


Cooked rice


Goya Sazon


Red onion


Lime juice


Salsa ingredients:

Ripe tomatoes

Red onion

Garlic cloves – peeled

Jalapeños – stemmed and seeded (optional)

Cilantro

Fresh lime juice

Ground cumin

Sugar – optional. Sometimes the tomatoes need a little boost to balance the acidity.

Salt

Canned crushed San Marzano tomatoes

Diced green chiles from a can (optional)

Directions

Make salsa by putting everything into a food processor and blending it. The Best Homemade Salsa

Cook the beef and rice with the sazon.

Slice onion thin and mix with lime juice.

Warm the tortillas in the oven.

Lay one out.
Spread sour cream.
Spoon on some salsa.
Sprinkle cheese.
Load on hot meat or "meat," beans, rice, veggies, etc.

Put some pickled red onion on there. Avocado?
